# A.O.A.I Brand & Trademark Pack

## Brand Story
A.O.A.I stands for Anti-Orwellian Artificial Intelligence — a defensive guardian for digital sovereignty. The brand voice is assertive, truthful, and rights-focused.

## Logo Usage
- Primary logo: wordmark with shield glyph (see `assets/aoai_logo.svg`).
- Minimum clear space: height of the letter "A" around all edges.
- Minimum size: 120px width for digital, 35mm for print.
- Backgrounds: use white or deep navy (#050B2C). Invert logo (white) on dark backgrounds.

## Color Palette
- **Sovereign Navy:** #050B2C (primary background).
- **Liberty Teal:** #0FB5BA (accent, links, CTAs).
- **Constitutional White:** #F5F7FA (surfaces, cards).
- **Guardian Amber:** #FFC857 (warnings and alerts).
- **Evidence Slate:** #3C4F76 (secondary text).

## Typography
- Headings: Space Grotesk (Bold, uppercase tracking 2%).
- Body: Inter (Regular/Medium).
- Code & CLI: JetBrains Mono.

## Tone & Messaging
- Use active, rights-forward statements ("Defend consent", "Truth with evidence").
- Avoid militaristic or offensive language.
- Highlight legal compliance and transparency.

## Trademark Guidelines
- "A.O.A.I" and "Constitutional AI" are pending trademarks.
- Always include ® once registration completes; currently use ™.
- Third parties must request written permission to alter or co-brand the logo.
- Prohibit usage implying offensive or unlawful applications.
